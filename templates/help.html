Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take? ‚ñº

We offer three shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days (FREE)
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days ($14.99)
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day ($34.99)

Orders are processed within 1 business day and then handed off to our shipping partner. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

What is your return policy? ‚ñº

We offer a hassle-free 30-day money-back guarantee on all purchases. If you're not satisfied with your order for any reason, simply:

  1. Contact our support team within 30 days of purchase
  2. Return the item in its original condition
  3. We'll issue a full refund once we receive and inspect the item

Return shipping is free for items that are defective or damaged. For other returns, the shipping cost is the customer's responsibility.

How do I track my order? ‚ñº

Once your order has shipped, you'll receive an automated email with a tracking number. You can use this number to:

  • Track your package in real-time
  • Receive delivery notifications
  • See estimated delivery dates

You can also track your order from your account dashboard under "Order History" ‚Üí "Track Shipment".

Is my payment information secure? ‚ñº

Yes! Your payment information is completely secure. We use:

  • 256-bit SSL encryption for all transactions
  • PCI DSS Level 1 compliance (the highest standard)
  • Tokenization to protect card data
  • Fraud detection and prevention systems

We never store full credit card numbers on our servers. All payment processing is handled by trusted third-party providers.

How do I reset my password? ‚ñº

To reset your password:

  1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password"
  2. Enter the email address associated with your account
  3. Check your email for a password reset link (check spam folder if not found)
  4. Click the link and follow the instructions to set a new password

For security reasons, password reset links expire after 24 hours. If yours has expired, you'll need to request a new one.

What payment methods do you accept? ‚ñº

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit Cards: Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
  • PayPal
  • Apple Pay
  • Google Pay

All payments are encrypted and processed securely. You don't need to create an account to make a purchase, though creating one helps you track orders and save your preferences.

Do you offer international shipping? ‚ñº

Yes! We ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. To check if we ship to your country:

  1. Add items to your cart
  2. Enter your shipping address at checkout
  3. Available shipping options and costs will be displayed

International orders may be subject to customs duties or import taxes (not included in our shipping costs). The customer is responsible for any applicable taxes.
